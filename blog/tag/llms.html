Gen AI test post

Gen AI refers to Generative AI, a category of artificial intelligence that is capable of generating new content, such as text, images, music, and other forms of media, based on input data or prompts. Unlike traditional AI, which typically performs tasks based on predefined rules or historical data, generative AI creates new outputs that resemble or are inspired by the data it has been trained on.
